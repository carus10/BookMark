Frequently Asked Questions

How does the subscription work?

When you subscribe to Premium, you'll immediately get access to all premium features including unlimited books. Your subscription will be valid for one year from the date of purchase.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your billing period.

What happens to my books if I downgrade?

If you downgrade to the Free plan, you'll still have access to your existing books, but you won't be able to add new books beyond the 1 book limit.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your Premium subscription, you can request a refund within 30 days of purchase.
